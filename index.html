Domain name registration

Choosing a domain is an important part of the website publishing stage, as it will affect the results of search engines and the number of website visitors will depend on it. Domain names can be registered by filling out the online domain name application form on the NIC website or by contacting one of the registrars, who will help you not only register the desired domain name, but also provide support in its maintenance and other issues related to the use of the domain name.

Selecting a maintenance company

In order for the website to be accessible to every internet user, it needs to be placed on a server. That process is called hosting (from English hosting - maintenance). Hosting is placing a website on the server's hard disk and this process allows you to ensure the continuous operation of your website on the Internet. If you already have a website design and content, the next step will definitely be choosing a hosting service provider. Some of the things to consider when choosing a company:

  • Service price
  • Availability and quality of operation
  • Server speed
  • Capacity
  • Security

Data placement on the hosting server

All website files and accompanying software must be placed on the hosting server. By default, website files are saved in the /www/yourdomain folder, unless specified elsewhere. It is recommended to use the /www/yourdomain structure and always leave the domain directory (Root folder) on "auto" when creating the domain name.

Home page maintenance

So that the website does not become a historical archive over time, regularly add new information, at least once a month write a few lines about your services or business sphere. Advertising is useful - sending information to your business e-mail list, sharing the news in social networks, as well as messages in your topic forums or interest groups.
